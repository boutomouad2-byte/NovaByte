About NovaByte Agency

Founded in 2015, NovaByte Agency has grown from a small startup to a leading web development firm with a global client base and a reputation for excellence.

Our Story & Philosophy

NovaByte Agency was founded by two visionary developers, Alex Morgan and Jordan Chen, who recognized a gap in the market for web development services that combined technical excellence with strategic business thinking. What began as a two-person operation has evolved into a team of 40+ specialists across design, development, and digital strategy.

Our philosophy is built on three core principles: Innovation, Quality, and Partnership. We believe that successful web projects require more than just technical execution—they demand a deep understanding of business objectives, user behavior, and market dynamics.

2015
